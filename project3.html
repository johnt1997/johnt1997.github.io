About

Dialogues Across Time

ALiVE brings history to life by letting users have natural conversations with AI-impersonated historical figures. Built on a sophisticated RAG pipeline, the system grounds every response in authentic historical documents while maintaining the unique personality and speaking style of each character.

Features

What Makes ALiVE Unique

A full-stack AI system combining document intelligence, conversational AI, and immersive visualization.

📚
Document-Grounded Knowledge

Upload PDFs and texts to create rich knowledge bases. Multi-strategy chunking (recursive, sentence-transformer, semantic) ensures optimal retrieval quality.

🧠
Personality System

Define character personalities with example Q&A pairs. The LLM adapts its tone, vocabulary, and perspective to authentically represent each historical figure.

🎧
Voice Synthesis

Hear history speak with Coqui XTTS v2 text-to-speech. Upload custom voice samples or use built-in voices for multilingual audio responses.

👤
3D Virtual Characters

Animated 3D avatars via ReadyPlayerMe and Three.js. Characters idle, gesture, and respond with lip-sync animations for a truly immersive experience.

🔍
Hybrid Retrieval

FAISS vector search combined with BM25 lexical matching for superior document retrieval. Relevance scores provide full transparency on source quality.

📦
Export & Share

Export entire conversations as beautifully formatted PDF or Markdown files. Perfect for educational settings, research documentation, and presentations.
